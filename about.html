About Us

Thaila Sannidhi was established on 4th March 2023 with a vision to manufacture pure and natural cold-pressed oils.

We extract our oils using the traditional Chakki (Wooden) extraction method, which is a slow and natural process. During extraction, no heat is generated, ensuring that the oil retains its original nutrients, aroma, and natural purity.

Because of this traditional method, our oils are 100% pure, aromatic, and rich in essential nutrients, making them healthier and more beneficial for everyday use.

Currently, we manufacture 15 different varieties of cold-pressed oils. In the future, we aim to expand our product range with more natural and high-quality oil varieties.
